Understanding CSS Pseudo-Classes

:hover Pseudo-class

The :hover pseudo-class is triggered when you move your mouse over an element.

Hover over me!

:focus Pseudo-class

The :focus pseudo-class is activated when an element receives focus (clicked or tabbed to).

:active Pseudo-class

The :active pseudo-class represents an element being clicked or activated.

:nth-child() Pseudo-class

The :nth-child() pseudo-class matches elements based on their position among siblings.
